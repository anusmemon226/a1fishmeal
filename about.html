About A1 Fishmeal

A1 Fishmeal is a dedicated fish meal manufacturer supplying export-grade fish meal from Pakistan. Unlike trading companies, we focus on direct production, enabling buyers to communicate directly with the manufacturer.

Our mission is to supply consistent-quality fish meal for aquaculture, poultry, and animal feed industries. For international buyers researching aquafeed raw materials (水产饲料原料), we offer stable protein content, controlled moisture, and export documentation support.

We work closely with importers, feed mills, and distributors in China and Vietnam who require dependable supply of imported fish meal at competitive prices.
