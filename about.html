About A1 Fishmeal

A1 Fishmeal is a specialized fish meal manufacturer (鱼粉厂家) based in Pakistan, supplying export-quality fish meal to international feed manufacturers. We work directly with buyers in China and Vietnam who require reliable imported fish meal (进口鱼粉).

Our operations focus on consistency, traceability, and long-term supply. Buyers researching aquafeed raw materials (水产饲料原料) choose us for stable protein levels and dependable export support.

As a Pakistani-origin supplier, we help customers looking specifically for Pakistani fish meal (巴基斯坦鱼粉) to diversify sourcing and reduce procurement risk.
