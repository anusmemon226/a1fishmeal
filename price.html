Fish Meal Price Information

Buyers frequently inquire about 鱼粉价格 (fishmeal price) and 鱼粉多少钱一吨 (how much does fishmeal cost per ton). Pricing varies based on protein level, raw material type, order volume, and destination.

For Vietnam customers, quotations are often provided as fish meal price per kg for easier comparison and budgeting.
