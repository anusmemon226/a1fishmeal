Buyers often ask about 鱼粉价格 (fishmeal price) or 鱼粉多少钱一吨 (how much does fishmeal cost per ton). Fish meal pricing depends on protein content, raw material type, quantity, and destination.

For Vietnam buyers, we provide quotations based on fish meal price per kg for easier comparison and budgeting.
