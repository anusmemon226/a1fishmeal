Applications of Fish Meal

Aquaculture Feed

Fish meal is a key aquafeed raw material (水产饲料原料), improving growth rates and feed efficiency in fish and shrimp farming.

Poultry Feed

Our fish meal for chicken feed supports healthy growth, improved egg production, and better feed conversion.

Animal Feed

Fish meal for animal feed is widely used in livestock and pet food due to its natural protein and digestibility.
