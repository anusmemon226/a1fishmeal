Applications of Fish Meal

Aquaculture Feed

Fish meal is a critical aquafeed raw material (水产饲料原料), improving feed conversion, growth rate, and health in fish and shrimp farming.

Poultry Feed

Our fish meal for chicken feed supports faster growth, improved egg production, and better nutrient absorption.

Animal Feed

Used widely in livestock and pet food, fish meal for animal feed provides digestible protein and essential amino acids.
